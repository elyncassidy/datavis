Television

Televisions are one of the most common appliances in Australian households, and their energy use depends on the screen size and technology.

Choosing an Energy-Efficient Television

When shopping for a new television, look for models with higher star ratings on the Australian Energy Rating Label. Features like automatic brightness control and energy-saving modes can also help reduce energy consumption.

Did You Know?

Switching to a more energy-efficient television can save you up to $50 a year on your electricity bill, depending on usage and the model you choose.
